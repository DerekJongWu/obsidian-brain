## Business Description

- **Problem:** Enterprise security teams are overwhelmed by 50M+ exposures from existing tools (Qualys, Rapid7, etc.) with no business context, no ownership attribution, and no scalable remediation path. Asset owners are often unclear across OS, application, and infrastructure levels.
- **Product:** Exposure management platform with agentic AI:
  - Agent-based system ingests findings from existing security tools
  - Hybrid on-prem/cloud capability (unique in competitive landscape)
  - Attributes business context by reading Confluence, ServiceNow, Jira tickets
  - Assesses exploitability by analyzing firewall rules, load balancers, cloud configs
  - Reduces 50M exposures down to 100K manageable items
  - Handles 500K+ assets (competitors break at this scale)
  - Identifies correct asset owners across OS, application, infrastructure levels
- **Wedge / entry point:** Replace traditional VMs (Qualys, Rapid7) and competing exposure-management tools; differentiate via hybrid + agentic mitigation.
