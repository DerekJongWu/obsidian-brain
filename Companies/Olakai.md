## Business Description

- **Problem:** Enterprises face rapidly growing AI spend (assistive AI subscriptions, agentic workflow token costs, developer AI tools) without visibility into ROI, cost attribution, or productivity calibration. CFOs are increasingly the budget gatekeeper as token costs scale.
- **Product:** Enterprise AI intelligence platform with three channels — (1) **Assistive AI** (ChatGPT, Microsoft Copilot usage tracking), (2) **Agentic AI** (agent workflow cost/ROI measurement), (3) **Coding IQ** (developer AI tool analytics — the primary wedge). Data sources: admin APIs (Anthropic, OpenAI), version control (GitHub), and direct integrations (Cursor, Claude, Copilot via hooks).
- **Wedge:** Coding IQ — addresses CFO budget concerns over developer AI token costs with project-level budget controls, alerts, identity mapping across GitHub/coding tools/Jira, and custom KPI thresholds for agents.

## Market & Competition

- Market evolution: moving from ROI justification (early phase) to cost governance and control (current phase, CFO-driven)
- Competitive positioning per founder: **business intelligence for AI** (not security / governance — distinct from semantic firewall / DLP plays)
- Tailwinds: token-cost concerns scaling with agentic adoption; identity mapping across developer toolchains is an underserved gap
