## Business Description

- **Problem:** $50B in social engineering losses last year, increasing with AI personalization. Current defenses failing — quarterly training ineffective, email protection exists but no coverage for LinkedIn/WhatsApp/iMessage. Attacks now highly personalized using AI to research targets' personal information.
- **Product:** Communication firewall sitting at the intersection of personal and work communication channels — covering the channels existing email and endpoint products miss.
- **Wedge / entry point:** VC-backed startups (YC ecosystem, investor networks, existing Quantstamp customers)

---

## Market & Competition

- **Market size:** $50B/yr in social engineering losses (as of 2026-05-12)
- **Key competitors:**
  1. Email security — Abnormal Security ($5B valuation, $250M ARR doing just email)
  2. Endpoint protection — CrowdStrike, Mandiant (miss personal device communications)
  3. Training companies — quarterly/annual training with limited effectiveness
- **Differentiation:** Focus on personal/work communication intersection — the missing piece between email security, endpoint protection, and training
