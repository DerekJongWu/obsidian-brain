## Business Description

- **Problem:** $50B in social engineering losses last year, increasing with AI personalization. Current defenses failing — quarterly training ineffective, email protection exists but no coverage for LinkedIn/WhatsApp/iMessage. Attacks now highly personalized using AI to research targets' personal information.
- **Product:** Communication firewall sitting at the intersection of personal and work communication channels — covering the channels existing email and endpoint products miss.
  - **Background screener (as of 2026-06-11):** scans WhatsApp, Telegram, email, and other channels.
  - **Small language model (SLM) runs on-device** (as of 2026-06-11) — NOT an LLM. False positive rate **0.46%**, recall **~95%**, scans in **~0.5 seconds** vs. 7–10 seconds for LLMs. Trained on real scam data, including attempts on Common Defense itself.
  - **Multi-channel coverage is the core differentiator** (as of 2026-06-11) — attacks increasingly cross channels (e.g. Discord trust-building, then iMessage execution). Legacy tools (Sophos, Microsoft Defender) missed the Humanity Protocol phishing email entirely.
  - **Deployment (as of 2026-06-11):** flexible — on-prem or Common Defense-hosted; messages never retained.
  - **Connector vetting (as of 2026-06-11):** WhatsApp, Telegram, Gmail, Outlook connectors require vendor vetting by Apple, Google, Microsoft.
- **Wedge / entry point:** VC-backed startups (YC ecosystem, investor networks, existing Quantstamp customers)
  - **ICP refinement (as of 2026-06-11):** 25–2,500 employees; easiest to close post-breach or post-near-miss.
  - **Key sales insight (as of 2026-06-11):** customers' most critical channels are almost never protected (Discord, iMessage, WhatsApp).
