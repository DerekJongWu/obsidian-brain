## Business Description

- **Problem:** Real estate tax analysis (especially cost segregation) is expensive, slow, and manual — traditional consultants charge $10–20K per study with long turnarounds; hard to scale across investment fund portfolios.
- **Product:** AI-native cost segregation platform. Classifies building components by depreciation schedule (e.g., carpet = 5yr, tile = 39yr) to accelerate tax benefits. Bonus depreciation allows 100% year-one write-off for <20yr components.
  - 80% automated as of April 2026 (up from 20% in December 2025)
  - Target: 100% automation on residential properties
  - Long-term vision: Model tax outcomes down to LP level for investment decisions
- **Wedge / entry point:** Sub-$5K AI-powered cost seg studies (vs $10–20K traditional consultants). Direct sales to investment funds with 10–20 property acquisitions/year.
