## Business Description

- **Problem:** Enterprises (and especially PE-backed companies) have fragmented structured/unstructured data across ERP, HRIS, CRM, AS400, flat files, and SQL — making it impossible for the CFO/OCFO to see strategic drift, headcount rightsizing opportunities, shadow spend, and AI-tooling substitution candidates without months of consulting work.
- **Product:** An AI layer on top of structured + unstructured data that automates internal ops across four pillars:
  1. **People** — headcount rightsizing, underutilized employees
  2. **Spend** — zombie contracts, shadow spend, vendor contracts, AI-tooling substitution
  3. **Projects** — strategic drift vs. business objectives
  4. **Agents** — agentic execution on the above
- Integrates with any backend system via ~20-click setup; caches + embeds in vector DBs; pulls only relevant slices (e.g., AP/AR/SO/PO from ERP, not the full system).
- Builds a knowledge base: org chart, spend map, business objectives, vendor contracts.
- **Wedge:** "Identifies zombie contracts and underutilized employees on day one" — immediate ROI framing for the OCFO.
