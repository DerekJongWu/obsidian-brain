## Business Description

- **Problem:** Most prospecting intelligence tools rely on money-in-motion events (death, divorce, inheritance). Advisor firms have one or two pieces of organic growth but no complete funnel.
- **Product Suite:**
  - **Convert** (prospecting): Pixel reveals up to 40% of anonymous website visitors with verified contact + wealth data; off-site intent monitoring of 6B+ signals; AI deep-research + custom email/LinkedIn outreach. 20-0 win rate vs Finny in head-to-head demos. Building agentic feature to automate prospect identification, copy, and sequence setup.
  - **Attract** (SEO/AI search): AI agent that gets firms found on Google, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Bing. Builds hundreds of SEO-optimized pages (location/service/comparison) structured for AI citation; automated compliance review; nothing goes live without approval; tracks rankings and refreshes content weekly.
  - **Multiply** (referrals): Digitizes Dan's IP for referral approach methodology.
  - **Living Sites** (upcoming): Direct competitor to FMG's template websites.
- **Wedge:** Convert tool drove first 44 paying clients; Attract upsell expanding ACV.

## Market & Competition

- **Direct competitor:** Finny (prospecting) — WealthReach 20-0 in head-to-head; Finny head of sales joining WealthReach in 2 weeks.
- **FMG:** Template websites + SEO; WealthReach SEO outperformed in pilots — "I see the demise of my industry" said one SEO-agency founder.
- **Money-in-motion tools** (death/divorce/inheritance triggers) — narrow data sources, single-funnel-piece solutions.
- Enterprise SEO opportunities with Dynasty, LPL (advisor websites audit).
