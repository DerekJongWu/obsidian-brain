## Business Description

- **Problem:** Traditional security-awareness training is generic and reactive; social-engineering attacks (phishing, voice, SMS) continue to bypass even trained employees. IT help desks are particularly vulnerable and lack scalable compliance testing (e.g., NYDFS).
- **Product:** Two main products:
  1. **Beacon** — replacement for security-awareness training using live threat intelligence
  2. **Hypnotic** — NYDFS-compliance testing for IT help desks (launching June/July)
- **Approach:** Live threat intelligence — open-source threat feeds (Center for Threat Informed Defense), Telegram chat monitoring, crawling live threat-actor communications, personalized OSINT-based simulations.
- **Wedge / entry point:** Help desk compliance testing as a wedge; offer free security-awareness training for 6 months to expand into Beacon.

---

## Market & Competition

- **Market size:** Compliance-driven (NYDFS) + security-awareness-training market
- **Key competitors:** Traditional security-awareness training vendors (positioned as replacement)
- **Differentiation:**
  - Live threat intelligence vs. static content
  - Proactive simulation vs. reactive training
  - Personalized OSINT-based scenarios
