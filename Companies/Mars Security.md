## Business Description

- **Problem:** Security telemetry is fragmented across SIEM, EDR, data lakes, and 50+ tools. Threat hunting is manual; detections are outdated before they're deployed; teams drown in unactionable data and silo'd queries.
- **Product:** Autonomous threat hunting and detection engineering platform.
  - **Federated search engine** maps data sources automatically, queries all with one interface
  - Ingests security telemetry across tools (SIEM, EDR, data lakes) via API
  - **Harvests threat intelligence from ~200 open-web sources**, auto-deploys detections against live threat campaigns and actors
  - **50+ integrations supported today, adding 5–10/week** (as of 2026-06-09)
- **What it is NOT (as of 2026-06-09):**
  - Not a SIEM replacement: explicitly positioned as a smart layer on top of existing stack
  - No SOAR/containment yet: alerts feed into existing SIEM or SOAR; containment and access revocation on roadmap but not current
  - No plans to host customer data; financially unreasonable at scale
- **Wedge / entry point:** Enterprises building or scaling threat-intel, detection-engineering, or threat-hunting teams that want a product instead of additional headcount.

## Market & Competition

- **Market:** Threat detection engineering + threat hunting — adjacent to SIEM (Splunk, Sentinel, Chronicle) but explicitly layered on top, not replacing.
- **Direct competitors (as of 2026-06-09):**
  - **Artemis** — playing the "same but not a SIEM" game; Mars is explicitly not going there
  - **Vega** (Gil Berstler's company) — aggressively targeting banks with a similar solution
- **Banks (as of 2026-06-09):**
  - Strong fit on paper (dedicated threat-intel, detection-engineering, and threat-hunting teams in-house)
  - Blocker: reluctance to connect a SaaS platform to so many data sources with cloud-side computation
  - Current focus is elsewhere given the friction; banks are not the primary near-term target
- **Budget framing (founder, 2026-06-09):** Organizations choose between headcount OR a product to mature detection capability; Mars is the product path. **Not replacing a line item, adding one.** AI-driven fear cycle is creating urgency and opening budgets right now.
