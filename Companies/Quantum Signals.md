## Business Description

- **Problem:** Top-tier hedge funds can build and deploy finance-native AI trading agents in-house, but thousands of smaller firms lack the engineering capacity to keep up. Building an in-house model requires 3–4 quants/engineers plus ongoing maintenance.
- **Product:** AI agents for intraday and macro trading decisions, powered by proprietary "Microstructure Foundation Model" trained on limit order book data from exchanges.
  - Covers futures, equities, crypto, FX markets
  - Currently: 4 equity indices (S&P 500, NASDAQ, Russell, Dow Jones); metals being added in next 2 weeks
- **Wedge / entry point:** Fraction of in-house build cost for equivalent performance; enterprise contracts starting at $12K ARR.
