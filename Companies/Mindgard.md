## Business Description

- **Problem:** Traditional pen testers lack AI testing capabilities; existing AI safety tools (model eval, guardrails, benchmarking) focus on offensive-content prevention but do not surface exploitable AI vulnerabilities.
- **Product:** Continuous automated AI security risk discovery, assessment, and protection
  - Uses 10 years of academic research from world's largest AI security lab
  - Combines offensive security expertise with psychometric profiling techniques
  - Only company actually surfacing exploitable AI vulnerabilities (not just safety issues)
- **Use cases:**
  1. CI/CD pipeline testing before production deployment
  2. Red teaming for continuous vulnerability assessment
  3. Pen testing enhancement (traditional pen testers lack AI testing capabilities)
- **Wedge / entry point:** Pen testers needing AI tooling; security teams running red team / CI/CD
