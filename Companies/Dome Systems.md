## Business Description

- **Problem:** Enterprises building AI agents face a control-plane gap — heterogeneous environments (AWS/Azure/GCP/Snowflake/Databricks/ServiceNow) make consistency, identity, security, and operational controls extremely difficult to enforce.
- **Product:** Integrated AI agent infrastructure platform with three pillars:
  - **Tool gateway** — MCP gateway for backend system access
  - **Model broker** — connects to any LLM provider (Bedrock, Azure, Vertex)
  - **Agent registration system** — works across AWS, Azure, GCP, Snowflake, Databricks, ServiceNow
- **Wedge / entry point:** Unified control plane sold to platform engineering teams (emerging "AI platform engineering" specialty). Enables both "AI out" (internal process automation) and "AI in" (external agent exposure) — producer-consumer model similar to Capital One's "Chamber of Secrets".
