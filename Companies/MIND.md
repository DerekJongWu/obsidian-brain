## Business Description

- **Problem:** Sensitive unstructured data sprawls across SaaS, endpoints, on-prem, and email with poor visibility; siloed point DLP tools miss data leaks and insider risk.
- **Product:** Unified DLP platform covering SaaS, endpoint, on-prem, and email — "four products in one." Multi-layer AI classification engine continuously discovers, classifies, and monitors sensitive data and autonomously detects/responds to events in real time. New AI-agent data-protection capabilities launching.
- **Wedge:** Focus on unstructured data only (not structured); unified platform vs. siloed point solutions.
