## Business Description

- **Problem:** Enterprises have countless repetitive desktop workflows hidden inside individual employees' day-to-day; automation today requires either explicit specs from employees or integrations into every SaaS the work touches.
- **Product:** Context mining layer for enterprises.
  - Desktop app that observes screen activity.
  - Screens PII.
  - **Requires zero integrations.**
  - Surfaces every repetitive task across the company with full context.
  - **Outputs automation blueprints** importable into Zapier, Make, Workato, etc.
- **Vision:** no task repeated twice on a computer; eventually expanding to mobile.
- **Founder insight from OpenAI:** OpenAI built datasets of people *typing* tasks, not *performing* them. Autostep builds the computer-use dataset instead — that's the moat.

## Market & Competition

- **Adjacent / partner ecosystem:** Zapier, Make, Workato — Autostep outputs into these tools rather than competing directly.
