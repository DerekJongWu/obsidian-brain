## Business Description

- **Problem:** Institutional asset managers' analysts and PMs spend their day across Bloomberg, browser, email, OneNote, and Word with no unified interaction layer; existing coding agents require users to act as software engineers and risk exfiltrating proprietary research/positions to OpenAI / Anthropic.
- **Product:** "Multiplier" — an AI agent harness that connects coding agents to a fund's files, data providers, and workflows entirely inside the customer's cloud. Replaces the interaction layer for browser, email, Bloomberg, OneNote, Word.
- **Wedge:** Fundamental discretionary L/S equity managers — high-touch, high-ACV, CIO-mandated (compliance requires CIO sign-off).

## Market & Competition

- **Market size:** 2,000+ fundamental discretionary L/S equity firms in the $500M–$5B AUM band; expansion vectors include natural gas, crypto, private credit, and family offices.
- **Key competitors:** [[]], [[]] — generic coding agents (Claude / OpenAI) that lack the anti-exfiltration architecture; Bloomberg (incumbent interaction layer + data backend).
- **Differentiation:** Anti-exfiltration security architecture inside the customer's cloud; no data exfiltration to OpenAI / Anthropic. CIO-grade compliance posture.

## GTM & Business Model

- **Buyer persona:** CIO at $500M–$5B AUM L/S equity manager (compliance-mandated buyer; CIOs are described as enthusiastic adopters).
- **Pricing model:** ~$15K per seat ACV on 12-month annual contracts; targeting $30K at renewal. "Per seat" is indicative for market sizing — platform is sold firm-wide, not per head.
- **ACV:** ~$15K → $30K (renewal target).
- **Contract structure:** 12-month annual; customer pays all inference and hosting costs → Withai's COGS is software only (100% gross margins reported).
- **Customer acquisition:** Primarily cold outbound; some inbound, no paid marketing. Mercator (energy-focused fund) came via prior relationship; others all cold.
- **Pipeline segmentation:** ~30/30/30 across "Claude afraid" (pitch security), "Claude mania" (wait ~6 weeks), and "Claude disillusioned" (easiest to close).
