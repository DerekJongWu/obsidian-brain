> [!info] One-Liner
> Torch Security is an AI-powered identity governance & administration (IGA) platform that automates connectivity to regulated applications, eliminating the need for expensive service integrators and enabling self-service IGA deployment with a secretless, zero-trust architecture.

## Thesis Fit

- Addresses massive pain point in regulated industries: 65% of organizations use less than half their IGA tool capabilities due to data connectivity challenges
- AI-driven approach reduces TCO by removing large professional services component (Deloitte-style implementations)
- Direct relevance to Fin LP base — regional banks are primary ICP
- Aligns with security/identity governance thesis

## Business Description

- **Problem:** IGA platforms sell capabilities but rely on service integrators for system connections; average regional bank has 100-150 regulated applications requiring connectivity
- **Product:** AI-powered IGA platform that streamlines the connectivity journey, enabling self-service deployment with secretless architecture and ephemeral credentials
- **Wedge:** Eliminates need for expensive service integrators; zero-trust authentication enhances security posture
- **Technical approach:** Secretless architecture with ephemeral credentials; AI automates workflows for disconnected applications; enables SSO and user access reviews

## Market & Competition

- Clear distinction between IAM (authentication/SSO — Okta, Ping Identity) and IGA (governance, compliance, lifecycle management)
- Torch focuses purely on governance/security side — no plans to branch into IAM/IT operations
- Targets security teams, GRC, auditing teams
- Competitors: Okta (governance offerings), Orchid — Torch maintains pure IGA focus
- ~12 employees (Harmonic, as of 2026-04-17)

## GTM & Business Model

- Hyper-regional go-to-market approach focusing on specific hubs:

- Target: Banks with 1,000+ employees (not credit union size)
- Serves both existing IGA customers and first-time/switching customers
