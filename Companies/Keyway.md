## Business Description

- **Problem:** Family offices struggle to source, underwrite, and manage commercial real estate — forced to choose between index-fund-style products (Blackstone) or broker-driven pipelines.
- **Product:** Normalized CRE data platform enabling programmatic asset purchasing, narrative/memo generation, rent rolls, and detailed financial analysis. Modular architecture for quick customization.
  - Originally built ML products for family offices to build CRE portfolios
  - Pivoted in 2024 to software licensing for asset managers and lenders
- **Wedge / entry point:** Software licensing to asset managers; expanding into lending workflows with banks and GSEs (Wells Fargo, Capital One, Freddie Mac, Fannie Mae discussions)
