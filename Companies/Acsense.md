## Business Description

- **Problem:** Identity systems lack recovery capabilities after breaches. Most vendors focus on prevention rather than remediation. The shared-responsibility model creates a gap: vendors secure the platform, customers secure the configuration — but most enterprises have no IAM disaster-recovery story. Identity became the #1 attack vector in 2023.
- **Product:** "IAM resilience platform" for backup, recovery, and compliance — real-time monitoring and configuration management; disaster recovery with standby tenant failover; compliance scanning for SOC/ISO/GDPR requirements. 3-2-1 backup architecture, Zero Trust principles, one-click recovery, continuous data verification, routine testing.
- **Wedge / entry point:** Backup/recovery for Okta tenants (currently supported); expanding into Microsoft Entra, Auth0, Ping One.
