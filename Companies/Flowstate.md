## Business Description

- **Problem:** Companies lack visibility into AI spend at the project level — existing tools (e.g., Ramp) only show high-level credit card spend, not structural decision-making data.
- **Product:** Platform that tracks AI spend attribution with two core layers:
  - Workforce layer: AI as labor doing tasks with variable costs
  - Economics layer: AI in product delivery to customers
  - Integrates data from JIRA, ADO, Linear (project tracking), GitHub (actual work), HR/payroll systems
  - Model optimization recommendations (e.g., using Haiku vs GPT-4 for appropriate tasks)
- **Wedge / entry point:** AI spend visibility and cost optimization for enterprises already spending significantly on AI tools.
