## Business Description

- **Problem:** Static benchmarking doesn't work for evaluating AI; evaluation is time-consuming and expensive (Adobe spent $500K+/quarter, Nike $2M+/project)
- **Product:** Multi-agent system with 5,000+ personas across cultures, ages, genders, job roles based on 800,000+ human-labeled data points. Two products: Compass (UX/quality evaluation) and Shield (safety/red teaming)
- **Wedge / entry point:** Free/community version for individual developers, expanding to enterprise SDK and on-prem
