## Business Description

- **Problem:** Most companies lack structured management infrastructure — ICs are promoted into manager roles without training, and existing tools (Fathom, Otter, Granola) only document meetings rather than improving how managers run them.
- **Product:** "Sage" — AI management assistant built on Talentism's proprietary management framework/corpus (decade+ of coaching data). Integrates with Teams, Slack, and Google Calendar to analyze meeting transcripts and communications; identifies when people are "talking past each other" in meetings; provides management-specific coaching and advice.
- **Wedge / entry point:** CHRO office (budget alignment with management training/coaching).
