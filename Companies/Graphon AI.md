## Business Description

- **Problem:** LLMs have hard context-window ceilings (~1–2M tokens); RAG approaches degrade with scale and multimodal data; converting video/audio/images to text loses fidelity. Enterprises with massive multimodal datasets (media libraries, claims photos, broadcasts, product catalogs) cannot effectively use them with LLMs.
- **Product:** Graphon connections — mathematical functions for studying large graphs — used as a persistent relational memory layer between enterprise data and LLMs. Replaces context-window limits with effectively unlimited memory. Native multimodal (video/audio/image/document) without text conversion. Model-agnostic; customers inject metadata and pick any underlying LLM. Actions engine for enterprise queries (e.g., "how many times does Levi's logo appear across game-day footage").
- **Wedge / entry point:** Paid POC ($15–20K), convert to multi-year contracts; demonstrate against RAG baselines on customer data.
