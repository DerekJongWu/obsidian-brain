## Business Description

- **Problem:** Mass-market retirement and financial planning is fragmented across advisors, brokers, and DIY spreadsheets — most consumers lack a single integrated planning view and the wealthy minority pay 1%+ AUM fees for advice they can't easily audit.
- **Product:** AI-powered planning platform with a deterministic underlying engine — runs forecasts, scenario comparisons, and personalized Q&A on each user's financial situation. Lab version auto-updates plans; future vision adds full agentic capabilities (moving money, opening accounts with human confirmation).
- **Wedge:** 80K+ paid subscribers, $250B liquid-asset coverage; deterministic engine + domain expertise + compliance moat against generic LLM competitors.

## Market & Competition

- Mass-affluent / HNW consumer financial planning + retirement
- $250B liquid assets on platform: ~$85B 401(k)s, ~$85B IRAs, ~$80B taxable
- Competitive landscape:
  - **OpenAI / generic LLMs** — emerging threat with general-purpose financial Q&A; Boldin differentiation = deterministic planning engine, compliance, security, domain depth
  - **Traditional RIAs** (e.g., Wealth Enhancement Group, Personal Capital legacy team) — full-service advice at 1%+ AUM; Boldin positioned as more scalable, software-first
  - **Robo-advisors** — narrower scope (asset allocation only) vs. Boldin's full planning surface
