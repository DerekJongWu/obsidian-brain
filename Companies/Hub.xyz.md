## Business Description

- **Problem:** Frontier AI models need rights-cleared, high-quality multimodal training data (voice, image, egocentric video) that is hard to source via traditional vendors at scale and with provenance.
- **Product:** Distributed, real-time data pipeline that aggregates raw multimodal datasets from a global community of 1,500+ contributors. Human-in-the-loop annotation/validation; instant API delivery; rights-cleared with full provenance. End-to-end pipeline (collection through annotation); image bounding boxes/detection/segmentation; egocentric annotation pipeline in development.
- **Wedge / entry point:** Boutique SMB egocentric capture (small shops, restaurants, bakeries, woodworkers) for robotics partners (Figure AI). Anchor enterprise customer Adobe.

---

## Market & Competition

- **Market size:** Multimodal training data (voice, image, egocentric video)
- **Key competitors:** Traditional data-labeling vendors (Scale, etc.)
