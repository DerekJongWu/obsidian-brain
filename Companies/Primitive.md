## Business Description

- **Problem:** Regulated financial institutions cannot deploy AI agents into production at scale without controls for accountability, traceability, and auditability of agent actions.
- **Product:** AI agent operating system for regulated FIs — deployment, governance, and performance measurement of AI agents within enterprise systems.
- **Wedge / entry point:**
- Platform architecture decomposed into three building blocks (as of 2026-05-19):
  - **Engine Room** — governance, guardrails, gateway (the "three Gs"); LLM Gateway with access to 19+ providers, live token-cost optimization, intelligent routing across models per task, custom SLM integration, multi-model agent architecture (different models for reasoning, response, memory)
  - **Assembler** — build/integrate agents and orchestrate them into workflows ("pods")
  - **Control Tower** — 7-layer drill-down visibility from executive to session level
- Four core capabilities framing the pitch: create, orchestrate, govern, measure agents (as of 2026-05-19)
- Banking/finance-specific prompt injection protection; PII/PCI redaction with custom regex rules at platform or agent level; parallel guardrail processing reduces latency vs sequential checking (as of 2026-05-19)

## GTM & Business Model

- **Buyer persona:** Banks managing "agent capital" at enterprise scale (as of 2026-05-19)
- **Pricing model:** Three-stage engagement (as of 2026-05-19):
  1. **Early Start Agreement** — $50K flat fee, no system/data access
  2. **Pilot Stage** — Master Service Agreement + SOW
  3. **Full Deployment** — License + development-team costs
  License pricing driven by number of pods (workflows/agents), transaction volumes and peak TPS requirements, platform access fee, and overage fees beyond contracted volumes
- **ACV:**
- **Contract structure:** Currently bundles all features vs modular pricing — rationale that the combined platform is more powerful than siloed components; microservices architecture would enable future unbundling if needed (as of 2026-05-19)
