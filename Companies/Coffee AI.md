> Coffee is an AI-driven CRM that automates sales processes (contact creation, activity logging, meeting prep) and positions as both a Salesforce/HubSpot adjunct and a credible long-term replacement.

## Thesis Fit

- Data lakehouse architecture enables superior AI pattern recognition over time vs. traditional relational DB CRMs

## Business Description

- **Problem:** Salesforce/HubSpot are overly complex with persistent data quality issues; reps spend too much time on manual data entry
- **Product:** AI-driven CRM that automates contact creation, activity logging, meeting preparation; built-in enrichment and call recording
- **Wedge:** Dual strategy — integrates as adjunct to existing CRMs (Salesforce Bulk API sync with custom field mapping) while offering a credible standalone replacement path; 30–60 min onboarding, staged sync approach
- **Technical differentiation:**
  - Data lakehouse architecture (tracks all data provenance, timestamps, previous values)
  - Automated data entry from email/calendar integrations
  - Permission mirroring: respects all Salesforce user permissions
  - Currently supports Salesforce and HubSpot; other CRMs based on demand

## Market & Competition

- Competing with Salesforce, HubSpot, and emerging AI-native CRMs
- Differentiated by dual integrate-or-replace strategy (most competitors only do one)
- Mid-market sweet spot: 30–100 Salesforce seats

## GTM & Business Model

- **Buyer persona:** Mid-market sales teams (30–100 Salesforce seats)
- **Pricing model:** Transitioning from per-seat to credit-based consumption model (similar to Cursor)
  - Core product free, pay for AI agent usage
  - Credits consumed for meeting prep, deal health monitoring, research tasks
- **Landing motion:** 5-person enterprise pilots → land and expand
- **Sectors:** Strong traction in technology and consulting services
