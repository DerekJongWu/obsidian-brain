## Business Description

- **Problem:** Insider risk is a judgment problem, not just a detection problem. Security teams are drowning in noise — average insider incident costs $16.2M and takes ~3 months to contain.
- **Product:** Complementary "judgment layer" that enhances existing DLP solutions (ForcePoint, Palo Alto, etc.)
  - Integrates in <3 hours vs 9-month typical DLP deployment
  - Zero-risk deployment — operates in parallel, not inline
  - AI brain with 95% accuracy in determining sensitive data
  - Vertical AI models pretrained for specific industries (finance vs healthcare)
  - Continues learning post-deployment from organization policies
  - Third-party app integration (Slack, Teams, SaaS) with out-of-the-box blocking
- **Wedge / entry point:** Gain trust as DLP enhancement, then replace incumbent DLP at license renewal
