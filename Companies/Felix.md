## Business Description

- **Problem:** Most professional-service work happens in email; pure agentic loops are inconsistent and token-expensive at scale. Existing automation tools are not approachable enough for professional service providers who want consistent, auditable outcomes.
- **Product:** Hybrid agentic-to-code automation:
  1. Claude performs initial task execution
  2. User reviews step-by-step outputs
  3. System compiles into a software workflow (not pure agentic loop)
  - Agents operate inside inboxes with attachment handling — users often don't recognize they're interacting with agents
  - Enables hundreds of thousands of runs without high token costs
  - Maintains consistency vs. pure-LLM approaches
- **Wedge / entry point:** Email-based professional services workflows (loan compliance, accounting reconciliation)

---

## Market & Competition

- **Market size:** Enterprise services automation; specifically large-organization accounting transformation
- **Key competitors:** Pure agentic automation tools; legacy RPA
- **Differentiation:**
  - Hybrid agentic-to-code: deterministic outputs after agent learns the task
  - Email-native — minimal user behavior change
  - Cost & consistency at scale vs. pure LLM agentic
  - Vertical focus on enterprise accounting
