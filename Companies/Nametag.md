## Business Description

- **Problem:** Enterprises can no longer trust identity signals in a remote, AI-driven world. 80% of cyberattacks originate from identity compromise. Legacy KYC tools were built for consumer compliance, not AI-powered impersonation. Critical workflows (helpdesk resets, onboarding, approvals) assume credential holder = right human.
- **Product:** Mobile-only human identity verification (~20–30s initial, sub-5s re-verification). Core use cases: helpdesk/IT self-service password/MFA resets, hiring/onboarding re-verification, step-up auth for high-risk actions. Mobile-native by design (device cryptography, secure enclaves) blocks browser-based deepfake/replay/injection attacks.
- **Wedge:** Helpdesk account recovery — acute pain, immediate ROI, expands into onboarding + step-up auth + contractors.
- **Integrations:** [[Okta]], [[Microsoft Entra]], [[Duo]], [[Workday]], [[Zendesk]], [[ServiceNow]]
- **Roadmap:** Expand to more enterprise identity moments → long-term: verified enterprise identity layer/directory reusable across vendors.

## GTM & Business Model

- **Buyer persona:** Security / IAM / IT (not compliance)
- **Pricing model:** Per-user (per employee), unlimited verifications — not per-check like consumer KYC
- **ACV:** ~$130k
- **Average TCV:** ~$230k (multi-year contracts)
- **Typical deployment:** ~14k employees per customer
- **Contract structure:** 2–3 year terms common; module-based expansion planned
- **Expansion levers:** Employees → contractors, add modules (helpdesk → onboarding → step-up), workforce → customer-facing identity (esp. FS)
