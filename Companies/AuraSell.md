## Business Description

- **Problem:** Enterprise sales orgs run on fragmented stacks — Salesforce/HubSpot plus 14–22 additional point tools — costing $2–3M annually with disconnected data and manual workflows.
- **Product:** AI-native GTM operating system with two deployment options: (1) full CRM replacement, or (2) operating-system layer on top of existing CRM. 220+ out-of-the-box integrations; unified data architecture combining structured CRM data with unstructured data via Snowflake/Databricks. Automates account preparation, messaging, prospect outreach, territory management, opportunity forecasting, and intelligent quoting.
- **Wedge:** Built by enterprise-sales operators (Harness, Twilio, Meraki, Nutanix, VMware lineage) rather than "CS grads with an AI wrapper."

## Market & Competition

- Replaces/augments legacy CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot) and the surrounding sales-tooling sprawl
- Differentiation: operator-built, 220+ integrations, unified structured + unstructured data layer
- Xerox case study: 70K accounts enriched + ICP/territory automation in 3.5 hours
