## Business Description

- **Problem:** Institutional investment managers (asset managers, OCIOs, family offices) run portfolios that mix liquid (public equities, fixed income) and illiquid (private equity, real assets) holdings on fragmented systems with manual data extraction and weak compliance workflows.
- **Product:** Multi-asset portfolio management platform with integrated trade management, compliance, and reporting. ML / automated data extraction for unstructured inputs (statements, term sheets). Secure cloud architecture.
- **Wedge:** Unified liquid + illiquid coverage on a single platform — most incumbents specialize in one or the other.

## Market & Competition

- Adjacent to portfolio-management incumbents (Clearwater Analytics, Addepar, BlackRock Aladdin) plus alts-focused systems (iLEVEL, Allvue, Dynamo)
- Differentiation: cross-asset (liquid + illiquid) unified workflow with ML-driven data extraction
- Rohit Mathur's prior leadership at Clearwater Analytics (data infrastructure / Prism / client reporting) is competitive intel
