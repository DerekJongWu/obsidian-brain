## Business Description

- **Problem:** $5T AI infrastructure buildout creates a massive new hardware attack surface. JP Morgan reports hardware attacks now account for 50% of all security incidents. Previously nation-state-level attacks are now accessible to broader threat actors.
- **Product:** Hardware-level threat detection, prevention, and supply chain risk management across PCs, servers, GPU clusters, network devices, and smart devices.
- **Wedge:** Pre-deployment hardware scanning before enterprise introduction; continuous monitoring (weekly → daily scans); patching for endpoints/servers expanding to all devices; hardware recycling/reuse validation between data centers.
