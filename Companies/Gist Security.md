## Business Description

- **Problem:** Manual security review processes (ServiceNow, OneTrust, intake tickets) don't scale with agile development and AI-driven changes. Each architecture review takes 40 hours to 1 month per change, and reviewers can't actually simulate the post-change architecture to assess risk.
- **Product:** AI-powered change governance platform:
  - Ingests from Jira, ServiceNow, Azure DevOps, design docs (Google Drive, Confluence)
  - Builds an asset-inventory graph database showing organizational relationships
  - Simulates post-change architecture to assess risk impact
  - Converts policies (PDFs, Excel) into mathematical logical expressions for automated testing
- **Wedge / entry point:** Replace the manual security-architecture-review process at organizations with dedicated security architecture teams — product security, third-party integrations, infrastructure configs, IT projects.
- **Expansion use cases:** SDLC security, access reviews, compliance automation, audit readiness.

---

## Market & Competition

- **Market size:** GRC tooling is multi-billion. Security architecture review is a narrower wedge currently handled in ServiceNow/OneTrust/manual workflow — the addressable spend is the people-time saved at the security architect layer.
- **Manual incumbents being displaced:** ServiceNow, OneTrust workflow tickets that currently take 40 hours to 1 month per review (as of 2026-05-18)
- **Differentiation:** Holistic risk view of the entire enterprise architecture (vs single-app or single-policy review); post-change architecture simulation; automated mathematical policy testing
- **ICP:** Organizations with dedicated security architecture teams handling holistic reviews — typically mid-market to large enterprise with mature security programs (e.g., Kaiser Permanente)
