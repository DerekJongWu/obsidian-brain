## Business Description

- **Problem:** Quant trading shops need fragmented tooling — separate reconciliation (Du Code, Triana), data, execution, ops — that takes 9+ months to assemble and 3–5 ops staff plus developers to maintain.
- **Product:** Infrastructure-as-a-service for quantitative trading operations:
  - Data platform + trade execution platform
  - 24/7 global support (US + India teams)
  - Real-time Level 3 market data globally included
  - Sub-10 microsecond OMS for listed assets only (as of 2026-06-02)
  - Streaming reconciliation, risk management, trade monitoring (as of 2026-06-02)
  - AI-powered unstructured data extraction — fundamental data from direct sources, web scraping for alt datasets, railroad data, prediction markets, PDF extraction (Jacob Kaippallimalil's prior startup tech, sold to S&P; as of 2026-06-02)
- **Value props:**
  1. Time to market: 6 weeks vs 9+ months typical setup
  2. Cost savings: 50% reduction on data licensing through enterprise deals
  3. Operational efficiency: Replaces 3–5 ops staff + developers typically needed
  4. "Hedge fund in a box" positioning (as of 2026-06-02)
- **Wedge / entry point:** Capital allocators ($4B+ AUM) with tripartite contracts (allocator + PM + fund cost sharing).
- **Addresses Millennium / Point72 exodus trend** — infrastructure for PMs spinning out of large multi-strats (as of 2026-06-02).

## Market & Competition

- **Market size:** Clients typically spend $20M+ annually on data — 15–50% cost savings on data alone justifies entire platform
  - Typical hedge fund data costs: $30–40M annually (as of 2026-06-02)
  - Enterprise vs per-PM licensing breakeven at 3 PMs (as of 2026-06-02)
- **Key competitors:** Point solutions — Du Code (reconciliation), Triana (one type reconciliation), Infusion ("eye bore" only); no unified incumbent
- **Potential adjacency:** Manager aggregation / performance analytics — similar to Canoe Intelligence (as of 2026-06-02)
- **Differentiation:**
  - Unified platform across all functions vs. point solutions
  - 6 weeks vs 9+ months time-to-market
  - 50% reduction on data licensing through enterprise deals
  - 24/7 global support included
