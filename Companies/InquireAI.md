> InquireAI is an AI-powered sales enablement platform for commercial insurance brokers, using a proprietary graph database of carriers, brokers, companies, and policies to drive prospecting, deep-dive account research, and automated policy comparison.

## Business Description

- **Problem:** 1.5M commercial insurance agents operate on outdated tooling (Excel) and lack visibility into carrier/policy history when prospecting or prepping for accounts
- **Product:** Sales enablement platform at ~$200/month per user with three value props:
  1. **Prospecting** — natural-language filtering across 1.2M US businesses (e.g. "every company in Texas with 5,000+ employees in healthcare offering supplemental insurance")
  2. **Deep dive** — comprehensive insurance history / carrier relationship summary on a target account for meeting prep
  3. **Policy comparison** — automates 8–10 hours of manual policy analysis; extracts quotes and docs from carriers
- **Wedge:** Repricing-risk intelligence layer sitting across the full insurance transaction graph

## Market & Competition

- Total addressable buyer base: ~1.5M registered insurance agents in the US
- Target accounts: ~1.2M US businesses as insurance buyers
- Competitive differentiation: proprietary graph database linking brokers, carriers, companies, policies, and ~10 years of transaction history; live agents monitoring executive turnover, news, and OSINT
