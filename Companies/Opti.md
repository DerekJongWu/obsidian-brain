## Business Description

- **Problem:** CISOs "hate SailPoint" — highest cost-to-value ratio in security tools. SailPoint typically connected to only 12–20 of 250+ enterprise applications. 20% of IT/security teams spend every quarter prepping compliance reports manually.
- **Product:** AI-native identity governance platform with specialized entitlement models and an identity workflow engine. Uses GenAI (specialized LLMs trained with NLP professors from Israel and Berkeley) expert in identity workflows — understands Salesforce entitlement hierarchies, SAP permissions, etc. Core question: "What access does Derek have vs what Derek needs."
- **Wedge / entry point:** Extend identity governance into applications #21–40 that SailPoint never connected to; eventually replace SailPoint entirely. Automate user access reviews and recertification for compliance-heavy teams.

---

## Market & Competition

- **Market size:** Identity governance is a top 3 CISO budget priority for 10+ years; incumbent SailPoint
- **Key competitors:** SailPoint (incumbent), [[Lumos]], [[Conductor One]] (startup peers targeting SMB with JIT focus)
- **Differentiation:** Enterprise-first vs. startup peers' SMB focus; authorization/governance not authentication (unlike Okta); 90% of deals currently non-competitive; JIT treated as a feature, not standalone business; GenAI layer specialized for identity semantics
