## Business Description

- **Problem:** Financial-services workflows (fund administration, KYC, CFO office) rely on extracting unstructured data from PDFs, emails, Excel and reconciling it across systems. Existing tools provide point automation but require heavy human-in-the-loop; vanilla agent solutions are expensive and unreliable at scale.
- **Product:** Knowledge-graph platform that ingests unstructured inputs, extracts/classifies/normalizes them into a purpose-built context graph per workflow, then layers agents on top for autonomous execution. Deployed on-premise within customer firewall on AWS, GCP, or Azure.
- **Wedge / entry point:** Replace the entire workflow (e.g., NAV computation, KYC, AP/AR reconciliation) rather than selling tools to existing operators — sold as a "virtual controller."
