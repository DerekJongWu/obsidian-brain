## Business Description

- **Problem:** Fintech / regulated finance companies have to enforce compliance across operational activity (product, engineering, design, marketing decisions) but lack any infrastructure mapping regulator judgment to real-time activity. Point solutions cover narrow surfaces (e.g., Blee for SEC marketing comms); foundation-model / observability layers can only solve 30–50% because they miss the regulatory relationship mapping and the judgment layer. Hallucination risk is unacceptable for compliance.
- **Product:** Three required components, all proprietary:
  1. **Regulatory knowledge graph** — proprietary per company, captures the actual regulatory obligations they face
  2. **Operational activity graph** — regulator judgment-based, captures the company's real-time operational activity
  3. **Judgment layer** — evaluates activities against regulations and produces a real-time compliance feed with system sources for each potential violation
- Integrates via **API or MCP server** — connects to project management, code, and design tools to monitor operational data
- **Wedge:** Infrastructural placement (vs. point solutions) + regulator-judgment-based ontology (vs. generic LLM/observability) + real-time feed with sourceable violations
