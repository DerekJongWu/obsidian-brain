## Business Description

- **Problem:** B2B payments are slow, manual, and communication-intensive. AR teams spend most of their time on collections, disputes, reconciliation, and cash application. Enterprises like Zoom run 30-person offshore teams to handle this work (~$500K/year).
- **Product:** An AR agent that handles collections like a human employee — not a co-pilot. The agent talks directly to customers via email, coordinates with internal teams, manages disputes, and runs reconciliation and cash application. Dispute management, reconciliation, and cash application have been in beta since March 2026. AP portal uploads were added in April 2026 (handles hundreds of portals for enterprise clients).
- **Wedge:** Start with AR (monetized) — the highest-pain, highest-willingness-to-pay surface for enterprise. Use AR revenue to fund an AP build-out, which will be free, in order to create network effects across both sides of the B2B payment.

## Market & Competition

- Borderless product, not regulated — US-headquartered, customers span US, Europe, and Singapore
- Enterprises currently solve this with offshore teams (e.g., Zoom's 30-person Philippines team at $500K/year)
- Long-tail collections is the initial enterprise wedge (Uber found Lunos via Gemini/Claude for long-tail collections)
