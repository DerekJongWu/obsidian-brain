## Business Description

- **Problem:** Enterprises spend ~$2.5–3T annually on transformation initiatives — half of total IT budget. The work is fragmented across planning, mobilization, execution, and monetization phases, with knowledge scattered across docs, spreadsheets, and PM tools. Most platforms in this space track status but don't do the work.
- **Product:** A platform for the full transformation lifecycle (Plan → Mobilize → Execute → Monetize) with three architectural layers:
  1. **Knowledge fabric** — integrates fragmented transformation data across the enterprise
  2. **Intelligence layer** — proprietary AI models plus LLMs for user intent
  3. **Work system** — structured workflows for business users (input requirements via agents) and technical SMEs (solution and detail projects)
- **Wedge:** Work happens *on* the platform — complex transformation work that historically took weeks/months can be completed in minutes. Differentiator vs. status-tracking PMO tools and generic LLM wrappers.

## Market & Competition

- TAM: $2.5–3T annual enterprise change spend (per founder, ~half of total IT budget)
