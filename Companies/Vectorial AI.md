## Business Description

- **Problem:** Traditional user feedback (interviews, surveys, A/B tests) is slow (2 weeks – 2 months), expensive, and biased. Product and marketing teams cannot iterate fast enough.
- **Product:** Human behavior simulation platform that generates synthetic users to predict and analyze user behavior, allowing teams to test product and marketing decisions before launch. Models can interact with products like real users and analyze marketing videos frame-by-frame.
- **Wedge:** Dual entry — product teams (simulate user testing of features) and marketing teams (simulate ad/video reactions and audience finding).

## Market & Competition

- **Differentiation:** Purpose-built for tech companies building digital products. 84% accuracy on opinion prediction (benchmarked vs 30,000 public reviews) vs ~56% for direct LLM approaches.
- **Competitors:** Simile, Aru — but those are focused on CPG / consulting use cases.
- **Data sources:** social media (Reddit, YouTube, LinkedIn, Twitter), enterprise user research data, product telemetry, human-in-the-loop interview enrichment.

## GTM & Business Model

- **Buyer persona:** Heads of Product, Heads of Marketing at tech / digital-product companies.
- **Pricing:** Platform fee + usage-based pricing. Planned PLG motion with 3 tiers — ~$50K growth-stage tier, $200K+ enterprise tier.
- **ACV:** $80–100K today.
- **Real-time audience finding** with weekly/daily signal refresh as differentiator.
