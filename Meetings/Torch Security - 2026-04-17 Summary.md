## IGA Market Problem & Torch's Solution

- IGA (Identity Governance & Administration) has a fundamental data connectivity problem
  - Platforms sell capabilities but rely on service integrators for system connections
  - 65% of organizations use less than half their IGA tool capabilities
  - Average regional bank has 100-150 regulated applications requiring connectivity
- AI streamlines connectivity journey, eliminating need for expensive service integrators
  - Reduces TCO by removing large professional services component
  - Enables self-service IGA deployment vs. traditional Deloitte-style implementations

## Product Positioning & Market Differentiation

- Clear distinction between IAM vs. IGA:
  - IAM: Authentication, authorization, credential storage, SSO (Okta, Ping Identity)
  - IGA: Governance, access certification, lifecycle management, compliance
- [[Torch Security]] focuses purely on governance/security side
  - No plans to branch into IAM/IT operations
  - Targets security teams, GRC, auditing teams
- Competitors like Okta, Orchid also doing governance but Torch maintains IGA focus
