## Product Strategy & Use Cases

- Three key use cases (not targeting IdP/authentication like Okta):
  1. Risk — overprivileged accounts, unused permissions
  2. Compliance — user access reviews, recertification
  3. Lifecycle management — joiners/movers/leavers
- Core technical approach: "What access does Derek have vs what Derek needs"
  - Same question applies across all identity use cases
  - Focus on authorization/governance, not authentication
- Enterprise-first strategy vs competitors targeting SMB
  - JIT is just a feature, not standalone business
  - 90% of deals non-competitive currently
- NHI (Non-Human Identity) as complementary feature
  - Agentic security trend emerging but market still early
  - Will add NHI visibility if customers demand it
