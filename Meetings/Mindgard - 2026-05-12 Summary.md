### Company Overview & Product

- [[Mindgard]]: AI-native penetration testing platform

  - Raised $8M seed at $28M post-money (406 Ventures led)
  - 1.1M ARR expected in next two months
  - Offices in London and Boston
- Product focus: Continuous automated AI security risk discovery, assessment, and protection
  - Uses 10 years of academic research from world's largest AI security lab
  - Combines offensive security expertise with psychometric profiling techniques
  - Only company actually surfacing exploitable AI vulnerabilities (not just safety issues)
- Use cases:
  1. CI/CD pipeline testing before production deployment
  2. Red teaming for continuous vulnerability assessment
