- Launched paying pilot December 2025, recently completed
- Founded by [[Sean Larson]] (sales/GTM background, real estate investor) and [[Reid Roman]] (ex-Google/Opendoor engineer)

- Cost segregation market: $3B annually
- Currently serves mid-market real estate firms with 5–7 year hold strategies; target customers acquire 10–20 buildings annually

  - Already in conversations with Tishman Speyer

## Pricing & Competitive Positioning

- Current approach: substantial discount to build trust and gain market entry
- Industry relies heavily on estimates and copy-paste methodologies
- [[LotLine]] advantage: computer vision + detailed property data = higher accuracy
  - Especially strong with developers who have exact cost data vs estimates
  - Can itemize from hundreds of pages of documents/photos
- Long-term strategy: premium pricing for superior accuracy/defensibility
- Target margins: 60–80% revenue profit per study with 90% automation
