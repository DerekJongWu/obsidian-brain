## Company Overview

- InquireAI is building a sales enablement platform for commercial insurance brokers at ~$200/month per user
- Targeting ~1.5M registered insurance agents in the US, most of whom still run on Excel
- Core thesis: a repricing-risk intelligence layer sitting across all insurance transactions
  - ~1.2M US businesses as potential insurance buyers
  - All brokers, carriers, products, transaction history, and deal economics
  - Proprietary graph database with ~10 years of broker/carrier/company relationships

## Product & Technical Approach

- Three primary value propositions:
  1. **Prospecting** — natural-language lead discovery (e.g. "every company in Texas with 5,000+ employees in healthcare offering supplemental insurance")
  2. **Deep dive** — comprehensive insurance history and carrier relationships on a target account for meeting prep
  3. **Policy comparison** — automates 8–10 hours of manual policy analysis; pulls quotes and docs from carriers
- Data foundation:
  - Government reporting data filed by insurance companies
  - Public company filing data with beneficial ownership structures
  - Reverse-engineered entity relationships (e.g. Gulfstream owned by General Dynamics)
- Live runtime agents:
  - Executive turnover monitoring
  - News scanning for business triggers
  - OSINT on industry changes
- Technical lineage: built on [[Anthony Beverina]]'s 25-year defense/intelligence ML background (terrorism algorithms, cyber security, healthcare social determinants)
