## Graphon AI — What They Do

- Core technology: "Graphon connections" — mathematical functions for studying large graphs, used as a persistent relational memory layer between enterprise data and LLMs
  - Replaces context window limitations (1–2M token ceiling) with effectively unlimited memory
  - Handles true multimodal data natively: video, audio, images, documents — without converting everything to text
- Founded ~7 months ago; Arbaaz Khan (CEO) built Amazon's Rufus chatbot from scratch, scaling to 65M queries/month globally
- Model agnostic — customers can inject their own metadata and use any underlying LLM

## Product & Demos

- Live demo: 1hr 13min London walking video — Gemini hallucinated (misidentified as GTA footage); Graphon returned exact license plate with timestamp citation
- Deep research feature: answered "second bus after Leicester Arms" with correct route number and timestamp — differentiating first vs. second instance, which most models fail
- Harry Potter demo: cross-referenced 3-hour film + thousands of book pages to surface specific differences between book and movie versions
- Actions engine: 49ers use case — CMO team queries how many times Levi's logo appears across all game-day footage
- Vanguard benchmark: Graphon 95% accuracy vs. RAG baseline at 62% on their America Saves report bot
