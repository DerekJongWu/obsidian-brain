## Company Overview

- Infrastructure for enterprise-grade computer-using agents, enabling AI agents to operate web browsers securely and reliably.
- Founder framing: "Fireblocks for crypto, but for computer use."
- Core value props cluster into two areas:
  - **Authentication & identity:** agents log in on behalf of humans, bypassing MFA, SSO, Cloudflare CAPTCHAs. Built a custom Chromium fork with 10,000+ patches to appear human-like.
  - **Action-taking:** proprietary "action index" built from hundreds of millions of learned browser interactions; lets agents be both dynamic and deterministic, covering the spectrum from full Playwright scripts to fully agentic tasks.
- Human-to-agent delegation is a core feature: agents learn from human browser demonstrations, then take over the workflow.
- Some customers are switching from API-based integrations TO browser-based specifically for observability and human-in-the-loop control.
- Horizontal infrastructure, vertically focused GTM (finance, healthcare, logistics, commerce). Finance is the largest vertical.

## Product & Technical Approach

- Custom Chromium fork with 10,000+ patches for human-like behavior — defeats Cloudflare and other anti-bot detection.
- Action index built from hundreds of millions of learned browser interactions.
- Spectrum from deterministic Playwright-style scripts to fully agentic loops — agents can mix both.
- Self-healing automation and managed Chromium instances.
- Self-hosted deployment available on customer cloud; margin-accretive vs. SaaS.

## Customers & Traction

- **Pay Clearly:** automated ~80% of accounts payable workflows for AT&T and similar enterprises, replacing offshore Philippines labor at ~20% of the cost.
- **Applied Pay:** connects to insurance carrier portals that were previously human-only, enabling data collection and process automation.
