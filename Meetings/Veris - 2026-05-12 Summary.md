### Company Overview & Product

- [[Veris]]: AI agent testing platform founded ~1 year ago
  - 10 engineers, SF/NYC based
  - Raised $10.5M Series A (Crew Capital, Susa Ventures)
  - Dorothy Chang (Flybridge) angel investor
- Core product: Simulation-based testing for agentic AI systems
  - Creates simulated environments (users, databases, tools) for agent testing
  - Procedural generation approach — generates API responses on-demand vs copying real data
  - Maintains conversation memory/consistency throughout testing sessions
  - Focus on enterprise use cases, not general-purpose agents
- Key differentiators from traditional ML testing:
  - Handles interactive, non-deterministic systems
  - Monte Carlo-style simulation at scale
  - Pre-production compliance checking for regulated industries
