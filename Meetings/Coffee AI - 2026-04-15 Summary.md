## Company Overview & Positioning

- Built to solve Salesforce/HubSpot complexity and data quality issues
- Unique dual strategy: works as adjunct to existing CRMs + credible long-term replacement
  - Most competitors either only integrate OR only replace
  - Enables gradual migration path for enterprise customers
- Core differentiators:
  1. Data lakehouse architecture (vs traditional relational DB) — tracks all data provenance, timestamps, previous values; critical for AI pattern recognition over time
  2. Automated data entry from email/calendar integrations
  3. Built-in enrichment and call recording capabilities

## Integration & Technical Approach

- Salesforce integration via Bulk API with custom field mapping
- Onboarding: 30–60 minute setup, initial sync takes few hours depending on data size
- Staged approach: pull data first, validate, then enable two-way sync
- Handles customized Salesforce instances; LinkedIn Sales Navigator integration
- Permission mirroring: Coffee respects all Salesforce user permissions
- Currently supports Salesforce and HubSpot only; other CRMs based on customer demand

## Pricing & Business Model

- Current: per-seat model
- Transitioning to credit-based consumption model (similar to Cursor)
  - Core product free, pay for AI agent usage
  - Credits consumed for meeting prep, deal health monitoring, research tasks
- Target market: 30–100 Salesforce seats (mid-market sweet spot)
- Strong traction in technology and consulting services sectors

## Financing & Growth

- $12M seed raised (includes Salesforce Ventures, HubSpot Ventures)
- Planning Series A late 2026 / early 2027
- Enterprise deployment strategy: start with 5-person pilots, land and expand
