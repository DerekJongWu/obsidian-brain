## Company and Product

- **Background:** [[Adam Hoffman]] founded [[Echelon]] after ~10 months at DOGE (Elon's efficiency initiative), then an incubation program at OpenAI.
- **Product:** AI layer on top of an org's structured and unstructured data, automating internal ops across four pillars: **people, spend, projects, and agents**.
  - Integrates with any backend system (ERP, HRIS, CRM, AS400, flat file, SQL) via ~20-click setup
  - Builds a knowledge base: org chart, spend map, business objectives, vendor contracts
  - Surfaces recommendations: strategic drift, headcount rightsizing, shadow spend, AI-tooling substitution

- **Data model:** caches and embeds in vector DBs; pulls only relevant slices (e.g., AP/AR/SO/PO from ERP, not the full system)
- **Expansion motion:** land with OCFO, then upsell horizontally across internal-ops functions
