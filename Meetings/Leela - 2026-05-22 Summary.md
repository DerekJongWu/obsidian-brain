## Product & Technical Approach

- Infrastructural compliance platform for fintech / regulated finance
- Three core components required:
  1. Regulatory knowledge graph (proprietary to each company)
  2. Operational activity graph (regulator-judgment-based)
  3. Judgment layer evaluating activities against regulations
- Product integration capabilities:
  - Integrates via API or MCP server
  - Connects to project management, code, design tools
  - Monitors operational data for compliance violations
- Real-time compliance feed showing potential violations with system sources

## Market Positioning & Competitive Landscape

- Target: fintech companies offering digital financial services
  - Initial focus: companies with regulatory obligations (bank-sponsored, credit, SEC-governed)
  - Expansion path: traditional regulated finance (hedge funds, PE, Vanguard pipeline)
- Differentiated from point solutions (like Blee — focused on SEC marketing compliance)
- Foundation models / observability companies can only solve 30–50% of the problem
  - Missing regulatory relationship mapping and judgment layers
  - Hallucination risk unacceptable for regulatory compliance
