## Product & Market Opportunity

- Agentic database operators replacing traditional DBAs
  - DBMS market: $170B today, growing to $330B by 2031 (15% CAGR)
  - DBaaS growing 19% CAGR, structured data growing 50% through 2028
- Core product: AI agents that proactively optimize database performance
  - Autonomous loop: monitor, diagnose, root cause analysis, test solutions on clones, implement fixes
  - Learns system-specific patterns (e.g., "Wednesdays are slow days")
  - Works across infrastructure (on-prem, cloud, air-gapped environments)
- Key differentiator vs legacy DBaaS providers:
  - Proactive vs reactive monitoring
  - Infrastructure agnostic vs vendor lock-in
  - Replaces DBA expertise vs requiring it
