### Product & Market Strategy

- Sage platform: AI management assistant addressing the lack of structured management tools
  - Built on Talentism's proprietary management framework/corpus (decade+ of coaching data)
  - Integrates with Teams, Slack, Google Cal to analyze meeting transcripts and communications
  - Identifies when people are "talking past each other" in meetings
  - Provides management-specific coaching and advice
- Target market: Tech companies and financial services
  - Primary wedge: CHRO office (budget alignment with management training/coaching)
  - Secondary expansion: Product teams (ICs promoted to managers without training)
  - Financial-services opportunity: large structured organizations with minimal management training
- Differentiation from note-taking tools (Fathom, Otter, Granola):
  - Focus on management infrastructure vs. task generation
  - Proprietary coaching methodology vs. generic LLM responses
  - Prevents bad meetings vs. documenting them
