## Company Overview

- Founded ~2 years ago in Palo Alto; ~30 people total (10-12 in Palo Alto, rest in Astana)
- Core thesis: task-specific models outperform general LLMs for enterprise workflows
- Architecture built on hypernetworks: metamodel generates weights on the fly for target open-source SLMs
  - Avoids LoRA's per-model overhead; no ML engineers needed per deployment
  - Self-perpetual feedback loop adjusts weights from task results
- Own inference platform (H100s) built in-house
  - Existing providers (Fireworks, Deep Infra) couldn't keep pace with new model releases
  - Enables full VPC deployments for privacy-sensitive clients (fintech, insurance, healthcare)
