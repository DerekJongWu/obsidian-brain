### Product & Market Position

- **AI-powered change governance platform** for enterprise security architecture reviews
- **Core problem:** Manual security review processes don't scale with agile development and AI-driven changes
- Current solution replaces manual intake processes (ServiceNow, OneTrust) that take **40 hours to 1 month per review**
- **Technology approach:**
  - Ingests from Jira, ServiceNow, Azure DevOps, design docs (Google Drive, Confluence)
  - Builds an **asset inventory graph database** showing organizational relationships
  - **Simulates post-change architecture** to assess risk impact
  - Converts policies (PDFs, Excel) to **mathematical logical expressions** for automated testing
- **ICP:** Organizations with dedicated security architecture teams handling holistic reviews
  - Product security, third-party integrations, infrastructure configs, IT projects
  - Example: **Kaiser Permanente POC → $500–600K deal in negotiation**
- **Expansion use cases:** SDLC security, access reviews, compliance automation, audit readiness

### Pricing & Business Model

- Scale the review process for every change in the organization — simulate the post-change architecture to understand the changes
- **ICP:** A security review team that is doing everything from product security, third-party integration, VPN decomposition, etc.
- **Main value prop:** Holistically looking at the risk architecture of the entire enterprise
