## Product & Technical Approach

- Agent-building-platform agnostic — covers every type of agent:
  1. Databricks and Snowflake agents (research / business specific)
  2. Agents customers build on their own internal agent-building platforms
  3. Agents sold by service providers (ServiceNow, Notion, etc.)
- Once agents are in production — identifies deviation in behavior vs. intent and deviation from governance policies
- Built-in catalog of AI agents plus enables creation of custom AI agents on Guard0 data
- Hyper-customization:
  - Entire platform deployable on-prem in air-gapped environments
  - Most SLMs do most of the work locally
  - Completely agentless — no proxy, no eBPF, self-service
  - Data collected is open — customers don't need to use Guard0 dashboards
- Customers can build custom workflows and reporting agents on top of Guard0's data
