## Company Overview

## Market Positioning

- Pioneer in a new cybersecurity category: hardware-level threat detection / prevention
- Coverage spans entire infrastructure — PCs, servers, GPU clusters, network devices, smart devices
- Complementary to [[CrowdStrike]] (deployed together at major banks)

## Product & Technical Approach

- **Core problem:** $5 trillion AI infrastructure buildout creates a massive new attack surface
  - 40 billion intelligent machines vs. traditional laptop-only environments
  - JP Morgan: hardware attacks now 50% of all security incidents
  - Previously nation-state-level attacks now accessible to broader threat actors
- **Solution capabilities:**
  1. Pre-deployment scanning of all hardware before enterprise introduction
  2. Continuous monitoring (weekly scans, moving toward daily)
  3. Patching for endpoints/servers (expanding to all devices)
  4. Hardware recycling / reuse validation between data centers
- **Customer integration:** multiple touchpoints across CIO/CISO levels; works with manufacturers, system integrators, partners — deep enterprise entrenchment beyond just security teams
