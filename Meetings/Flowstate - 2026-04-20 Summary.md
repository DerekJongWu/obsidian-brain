## Product & Market Position

- Platform tracks AI spend attribution at project level, not just credit card statements
- Two core layers:
  - Workforce layer: AI as labor doing tasks with variable costs
  - Economics layer: AI in product delivery to customers
- Data sources integration: JIRA, ADO, Linear (project tracking), GitHub (actual work), HR/payroll systems (blended human-AI reality)
- Competitive differentiation vs Ramp's AI spend tracking:
  - Goes deeper into structural decision-making
  - Project-level attribution vs high-level spend visibility
  - Future roadmap includes inter-agent payments and autonomous transactions
- Model optimization recommendations (e.g., using Haiku vs GPT-4 for appropriate tasks)
