## Product and Technology

- **Background screener for scams** across WhatsApp, Telegram, email, and other channels.
- **Small language model (SLM) runs on-device**, not an LLM.
  - **False positive rate: 0.46%**; **recall: ~95%**.
  - Scans in **~0.5 seconds** vs. 7–10 seconds for LLMs.
  - Trained on real scam data, including attempts on Common Defense itself.
- **Multi-channel coverage is the core differentiator.**
  - Attacks increasingly cross channels (e.g. Discord trust-building, then iMessage execution).
  - Legacy tools (**Sophos, Microsoft Defender**) missed the Humanity Protocol phishing email entirely.
- **Deployment is flexible:** on-prem or Common Defense-hosted; messages never retained.
- **Connectors** (WhatsApp, Telegram, Gmail, Outlook) require vendor vetting by Apple, Google, Microsoft.
