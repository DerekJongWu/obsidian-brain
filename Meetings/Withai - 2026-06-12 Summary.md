## Company and Product

- [[Withai]] is an AI agent harness ("Multiplier") for institutional asset managers
  - Launched last month; 3 funds on platform, **$155K ARR**, **100% gross margins**
  - Average user spends **4+ hours/day** on platform
- Core value prop: connects coding agents to a fund's files, data providers, and workflows without requiring users to act as software engineers
  - Replaces interaction layer for browser, email, Bloomberg, OneNote, Word
  - Fully secured inside customer's cloud; no data exfiltration to OpenAI or Anthropic
- **Key differentiator:** anti-exfiltration security architecture (hardest element to replicate)
- **ICP:** fundamental discretionary long/short equity managers, $500M to $5B AUM
  - 2,000+ firms in this micro-vertical; expansion vectors: natural gas, crypto, private credit, family offices
  - Final buyer is the CIO (compliance requires it); CIOs are enthusiastic adopters
